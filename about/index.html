Mission

At my core, I am a problem solver.

I traveled a non-linear path, which was paved through relationship building and curiosity. While the majority of my work focuses on finding ways to implement technology, people are the reason that I am exploring the work.

Even though the nature of work is changing, I am optimistic that people will always remain at the center of it. It is my hope that I continue to benefit from working with curious people who aspire to explore interesting things.
