Hello, I'm Matt Brigidi

I am a data analytics professional with over a decade of experience working in digital media.

My current title is the Director of Innovation & Solutions at Condé Nast. I've spent time working in Content, Finance, Operations, & Strategy and have enjoyed learning about how a modern business is structured.

In my free time, I enjoy academic pursuits — regardless of whether I am the student or the teacher. You can find some examples of my work in the Tutorials section of this site or by visiting my YouTube channel.

I am passionate about problem solving and informed decision making. I aspire to have my days filled with opportunity rather than obligation because I think an opportunity is something you get to do; while an obligation is something you have to do.

Please reach out if you have an idea that you're excited about — I love collaborating!

Mission

At my core, I am a problem solver.

I traveled a non-linear path, which was paved through relationship building and curiosity. While the majority of my work focuses on finding ways to implement technology, people are the reason that I am exploring the work.

Even though the nature of work is changing, I am optimistic that people will always remain at the center of it. It is my hope that I continue to benefit from working with curious people who aspire to explore interesting things.
